Over the Counter Medication App

Adolescents often lack guidance in self-administering common over-the-counter medications like Tylenol and Advil, relying on parental advice through phone calls. To foster independence and ensure safe self-care practices, there is a need for an Over-the-Counter Medication App. This app will enable adolescents to input symptoms, receive personalized medication suggestions, and receive reminders for timely doses, promoting responsible medication management among the youth.

Affinity Diagram: Cluster Ideas

This affinity diagram is a cluster of ideas. The ideas are split into categories: product, hurdles, promotions, adverting, and shipping. The product ideas include products with its main purpose to help/assist the customer. Some of these ideas are hurdles. These hurdles include regulation issues, customer issues, and additional costs. The other ideas are about advertising and promotions, which they include ways on how to sell the product and provide promotions for our customers. Lastly, we have shipping ideas, which includes ways we can ship out our products to the customer.
